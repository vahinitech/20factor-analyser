VahiniYour Six-Week Plan
A gentle path forward

The growth plan

Page 09

Small, playful practice beats long sessions. Each fortnight targets one focus area. Ten minutes, three times a week, is plenty.

Weeks 1–2

Even & steady

  • “Two-finger” height: keep every body letter the same size
  • Write five words, then check they match in height
  • Rest the forearm fully so the hand glides
Weeks 3–4

Room to breathe

  • Stand a “matchstick gap” between letters
  • Lengthen the tails of g, y and p below the line
  • Stop two lines early to leave a footer margin
Weeks 5–6

Smooth flow

  • Write to a slow 1-2-3 count per word for even pressure
  • Glance ahead so words fit before the right edge
  • Re-test with the IMU pen and compare the scores
